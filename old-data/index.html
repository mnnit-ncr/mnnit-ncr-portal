MONERECO (MNNIT) ALUMNI ASSOCIATION- Delhi NCR was formed in the year 1988-89 in faridabad by fellow monerecons / executive committee members.
- Er. Anil Uppal (74-M) served as it's founder President till Sep'14, followed by Er. R. N. Gulati (70-M) till Nov'23, Er. Ramesh Thakur (75-M) till Jan'26, currently presided by Er. M. P. Arya (75-M). Today it's a strong family of 600+ life members.

- We do meet in Delhi / Faridabad / Noida / Gurgaon 2-3 times for individual meet & once a year for a gala annual family get together with games & cultural events followed by cocktail-lunch / dinner.
- The Association provides a platform to fellow monerecons in Delhi NCR Region to stay connected & to be of help to each other in individual / collective capacity.
- To become a life member one has to pay Rs. 2000 (non-refundable), by cash or a/c payee cheque in favour of 'MONERCO ALUMNI ASSOCIATION' in one of the get-togethers.
- Non-life members are requested to become life members at the earliest to strengthen the Association.

WhatsApp Groups: We had 4 groups (G1, G2, G3, G4) earlier when group capacity was limied to 256. [G1-G2-G3 for Life members, and G4 for non-life members, who joined one of the get togethers or requested to be added in the group to keep receiving important communications.], subsequently WA group capacity got increased, and we retained only G1 (for life members) and G4 (for non-life members). 'Only Admins can post' settings applied to avoid Good Morning messages, generic forwards etc.

Google Group:'MNNIT (Monereco) Alumni Association - Delhi NCR', Group email-id is: mnnit-delhi-ncr@googlegroups.com: Group is configured to receive emails only from the group members.
Sincere thanks to all the Motis / Pearls for making the Get-together Enjoyable and Memorable. A hearty welcome to new life members becoming integral part of Monereco-NCR family.
